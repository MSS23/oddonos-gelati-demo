Our Journey

2004

The Founder's Inspiration

Christian Oddono, missing the taste of his childhood spent at his first Oddono's Gelato Italiano in South Kensington, bringing authentic gelato to London. Born from his grandmother's secret recipes and a mission to offer fresh, natural Italian gelato in London.

2005

Commitment to Ingredients

We established our core principle: to use only the finest natural ingredients, sourced directly from their point of origin. From vanilla pods from Madagascar to pistachios from Sicily, creating unparalleled quality.

2008

The Artisanal Process

Perfecting our craft, we standardized our in-house, truly artisanal production process, guaranteeing freshness and flavor in every scoop.

2012

Our Growth

With a growing family of loyal customers, Oddono's expands across London, sharing the passion for true Italian gelato with new neighborhoods.

Today

7 Locations & Growing

Now with 7 shops across London and wholesale partnerships throughout the UK, we continue to craft over 130 unique flavors, staying true to our founding principles while innovating for the future.

Our Ingredients & Process

Quality is our obsession. We source the finest natural ingredients from around the world and make our gelato from scratch with individually added ingredients.

Premium Ingredients

Vanilla pods from Madagascar, pistachios from Sicily, hazelnuts from Piedmont, and chocolate from the finest sources. We never compromise on quality.

Made Fresh Daily

All gelato is made daily in our South Kensington "Artisan Laboratory" using in-house recipes. We make gelato from scratch with individually added ingredients.

100% Natural

We do not use any colorings, preservatives, or GM products. Just pure, natural ingredients that you can taste in every spoonful.

130+ Flavors Created

From beloved classics to innovative tastes, we've crafted over 130 unique gelato flavors. Our best sellers include Chocolate, Nocciola (hazelnut), Bacio (chocolate-hazelnut), and Pistachio, while fruity sorbets like Mango, Strawberry, and Coconut are also crowd favorites.

Classic Flavors

Special Dietary Options

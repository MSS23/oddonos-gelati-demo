Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy Oddono's gelato?

We have 7 shops across London. Visit our Shops page to find your nearest location.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! You can order Oddono's through delivery partners like UberEats, Deliveroo, and Just Eat from participating locations.

Do you have dairy-free options?

Absolutely! We offer dairy-free (soy-based) gelato and a variety of fruit sorbets. Ask our staff for current dairy-free flavors available.

Can I order a gelato cake?

Yes! We create beautiful gelato cakes for special occasions. Contact us directly or visit any of our shops to place an order.

How do I activate my Gelato Card?

Visit our Gelato Card page for detailed instructions on how to activate and use your loyalty card.

Do you cater for events?

Yes! We provide gelato carts and catering for weddings, corporate events, and parties. Contact us at info@oddonos.com for more information.

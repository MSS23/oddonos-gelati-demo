Oddono's gelato is made using traditional Italian methods with 100% natural ingredients. Unlike regular ice cream, our gelato has less fat, is churned slower for a denser texture, and is served at a warmer temperature to enhance the flavors. We never use artificial colorings, preservatives, or GM products.
